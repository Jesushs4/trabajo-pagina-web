CHARMS

There are 40 charms that you can collect during the game.

Every charm requires a certain number of notches to be equipped depending on the charm.

The player starts with 3 notches, which can be upgraded to 11 notches by finding them around the map.


You can combine charms in different ways as long as you have the necessary notches.

These are the most common builds in the game:
